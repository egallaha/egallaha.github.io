Commission Terms

When you commission me for personal art, you agree to the following terms and services:

  1. The client may use the commissioned art for personal use only. The client may not use the art received for commercial or for-profit purposes.
  2. The client has full rights to edit the image, use it in banners, etc. etc. as long as it is not used in anything political, hate speech, or used to slander the artist’s personal views.
  3. The original art belongs to the original artist (TGI Studios LLC) and the rights can be revoked if any of the terms listed are violated.
  4. The characters commissioned, however, will always belong to their original creator.
  5. If the client is not satisfied with the artwork they receive, they have the full right to request changes 30 days after the art’s completion, free of charge. After the 30 days any edits will require additional charges.
  6. All purchases are final and there are no refunds after the artwork is finished.
